About Us

A finance partner rooted in your community

Trimurti Fincap Private Limited is an RBI-registered Non-Banking Financial Company committed to making credit simple, fair, and accessible.

Who we are

Trimurti Fincap Private Limited is a Non-Banking Financial Company (NBFC) registered with the Reserve Bank of India (RBI Reg. No. B.01.00432). We provide personal, business, vehicle, gold, property, education and MSME loans to individuals, families, and small businesses across Gujarat.

We believe finance should feel like a helping hand, not a hurdle. Many hard-working people — shop owners, traders, salaried families, farmers, and first-time borrowers — find it difficult to get timely credit from big banks. Trimurti Fincap exists to fill that gap: honest guidance, clear terms, local understanding, and support at every step. From families planning life's big moments to small businesses chasing growth, we serve our community with credit that is responsible and easy to access.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and small businesses in Gujarat with responsible, easy-to-access credit that helps them grow.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted community lender in Gujarat — the first name people think of when they need honest finance.

Our Promise

Complete transparency, fair interest rates, no hidden charges, and respect for every customer at every step.

Our Regulation

Registered NBFC (RBI Reg. No. B.01.00432) operating fully under Reserve Bank of India guidelines and fair practice code.
